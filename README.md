# PCOSense

PCOSense – AI-Powered PCOS Detection & Health Assistance Platform

PCOSense is an intelligent healthcare platform designed to assist in the early detection and management of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) using Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Generative AI.

The system enables users to assess their PCOS risk by entering health-related parameters such as age, BMI, menstrual cycle details, symptoms, and ultrasound results. Using an ML-based prediction engine (XGBoost-ready architecture), the platform provides instant PCOS risk assessment with confidence scores.

Beyond prediction, PCOSense offers a complete health support ecosystem by providing personalized diet recommendations, prediction history tracking, awareness resources, and an AI-powered chatbot for user guidance.

# Key Features
1] Secure user authentication and authorization using JWT
2] AI-powered PCOS risk prediction using medical and symptom-based inputs
3] Confidence-based prediction results
4] Prediction history management and tracking
5] Personalized diet plan generation based on health data
6] AI chatbot for PCOS-related queries and health guidance
7] PCOS awareness and educational resources
8] Responsive and user-friendly frontend interface

# Project Goal

The goal of PCOSense is to promote early detection, awareness, and better health management for women at risk of PCOS through accessible AI-driven healthcare solutions.

By combining predictive analytics with intelligent health assistance, PCOSense aims to provide users with actionable insights and personalized recommendations to support healthier lifestyles.
